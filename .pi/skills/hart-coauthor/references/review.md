# Post review protocol

Review in this order.

## Structure

- Does the opening establish the subject and payoff?
- Does each section unlock the next?
- Are removed sections still referenced by the introduction or conclusion?
- Does the conclusion answer the opening incentive?

## Claims

- Separate observation, inference, speculation, prescription, and implementation detail.
- Narrow or support broad claims.
- Find hidden assumptions, false dichotomies, category errors, circular reasoning, and overgeneralization.
- Verify that claims about Raj's experience came from Raj.

## Models

- Check equations against the prose and implementation.
- Check Mermaid edge direction, labels, node roles, and abstraction level.
- Prefer relations as edges unless the relation needs identity, state, provenance, or properties.
- Remove formalism that does not compress or clarify structure.

## Voice

- Keep speaker attribution explicit.
- Preserve Raj's authored annotations.
- Keep H.A.R.T. humor grounded in observable work.
- Remove generic assistant phrasing and inflated conceptual language.

## Continuity

- Check internal links and archive links.
- Identify ontology terms worth reusing.
- Flag inconsistencies with earlier posts.

## Mechanics

- Check heading hierarchy and section separators.
- Check annotation references and bodies.
- Build with the local SSG.
